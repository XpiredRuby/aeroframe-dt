# Production Solid/Contact Model — Planned Contract

The production model will not be built by copying the benchmark model. It must record:

- geometry revision and simplifications;
- element formulations and quality limits;
- contact pairs, normal formulation, friction law, pin/bolt idealization, preload, and clearances;
- load introduction and remote/coupling definitions;
- material model and source IDs;
- nonlinear increments, convergence controls, residual histories, and stabilization energy;
- reaction-force and moment equilibrium;
- mesh family and extraction rules;
- integrated bearing/contact resultants, fastener loads, area/path-averaged stresses, and structural hot-spot quantities;
- excluded singular peaks and rationale.
