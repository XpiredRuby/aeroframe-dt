# Material and Fastener Trade — Initial Candidates

No design allowable is released in this file. Values must be product-form-, thickness-, direction-, temperature-, and statistical-basis-specific.

| Candidate | Intended trade role | Key questions before selection |
|---|---|---|
| 7050-T7451 aluminum | low-density baseline fitting candidate | forging/plate availability, ST/L allowables, corrosion and bearing performance, thickness effects |
| Ti-6Al-4V annealed | high specific strength/corrosion alternative | galling, machining, bearing behavior, procurement form, galvanic compatibility |
| 15-5PH stainless steel | compact/high-strength corrosion-resistant alternative | density penalty, heat-treatment condition, fracture toughness, corrosion environment |

Fasteners will be traded between close-tolerance titanium and high-strength alloy-steel aerospace families. Exact NAS/MS/AS specification selection is source-gated.
