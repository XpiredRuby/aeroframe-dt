# Geometry Concept

The authoritative concept is a representative forward pylon attachment fitting with a pin-loaded lug/clevis interface and a fastened wingbox flange.

## Frozen topology

- one primary lug axis approximately lateral;
- a load-carrying web/body between lug and flange;
- a multi-fastener wingbox flange;
- left/right fitting pair at the station;
- replaceable dimensions controlled through the parameter schema;
- no decorative aircraft-exterior detail.

## Not frozen

- hole diameter;
- lug width/thickness;
- edge distance;
- flange thickness and footprint;
- fastener count/pattern;
- blend radii;
- local wingbox thickness/stiffness.

These dimensions remain blank until context CAD is available or a representative design envelope is formally approved in `docs/DECISIONS.md`.
