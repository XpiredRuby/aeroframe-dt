# GD&T and PMI Requirements

The initial datum strategy is functional and will be verified against assembly and inspection needs:

- Datum A: wingbox-interface mounting plane.
- Datum B: primary pin/lug axis.
- Datum C: clocking/side face that removes remaining rotation.

Required PMI categories:

- basic location and position of the primary lug hole relative to A|B|C;
- fastener-hole pattern position relative to the datum reference frame;
- profile of transition surfaces only where functionally necessary;
- perpendicularity/parallelism of interface surfaces where they control load introduction;
- flatness of the mounting plane;
- hole size and fit class after the fastener/pin specification is selected;
- surface texture at bearing and mounting interfaces;
- material, product form, heat treatment, coating, and inspection notes.

No numerical tolerance is frozen until the tolerance stack and process/inspection capability are modeled.
